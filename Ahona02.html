Jayma's restaurant

A Bengali eatery in New York

Fuchka$6.99
There is something comfortingly familiar about eating a fuchka-These crispy, hollow shells filled with a burst of tangy tamarind water, spicy mashed potatoes, and chickpeas create a flavor explosion in every bite.

Jhalmuri $5.99
Spicy and tangy Jhalmuri is a tasty mix of puffed rice, crunchy peanuts, flavorful spices, and fresh veggies. It's a yummy street snack with a burst of flavors, perfect for those who crave a deliciously spiced adventure that will leave you craving for more.

Chicken Briyani $11.99
Biryani is well-known for its flavor and aroma. Fresh herbs, saffron, and basmati rice give it a sweet-floral scent that you may smell before you taste. Warmth and subtle heat are provided with whole garam masala, fried onions, and marinated beef. It tastes like a savory, prepared rice pilaf in general.

Payesh $8.99
Payesh is a delightful rice pudding, sweetened with aromatic cardamom and enriched with creamy milk. A cherished dessert, sprinkled with nuts, offering a taste of traditional comfort in every spoonful.

Malai Cha $2.99
A rich and creamy tea delight, blending smooth malai (cream) with fragrant cha (tea). Indulge in the comforting warmth of this luscious beverage for a delightful taste experience.
